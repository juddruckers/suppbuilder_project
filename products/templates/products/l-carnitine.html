Acetyl-L Carnitine Benefits

Clinical resarch on Acetyl-L Carnitine supplementation has shown the following benefits.

  • Decrease Fat mass

    Fat mass is is simply the percentage of fat your body contains

  • Decrease mental and physical fatigue

    Fatigue is the lack motivation, energy and vitality.

  • Decrease lactate production

    Lactate can be produced during prolonged exercise, and while it is not thought to contribute to muscular failure it seems to be a good biomarker of it. Reductions in lactate production tend to be associated with prolonged physical endurance.

  • Decrease muscle damage

    Muscle damage refers to the breaking of muscle tissue during exercise. Reducing muscle damage may alleviate soreness from exercise.
