Creatine Benefits

Creatine supplementation has shown the following benefits.

  • Increase Power Output and Strength

    Power Output is the ability to procure a large amount of strength in a rapid manner

  • Improve Anaerobic capacity

    Anaerobic capacity is the maximal work performed during maximum-intensity short-term physical effort such as weightlifting, HIIT, and sprinting.

  • Increase lean mass

    Lean Mass is overall body mass minus fat mass.

  • Improve muscular endurance

    Muscular endurance tends to refer to the capacities of a muscle tissue to continuously exert itself during one 'set' of exercises
